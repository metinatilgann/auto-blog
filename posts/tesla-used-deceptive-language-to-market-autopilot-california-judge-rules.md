Tesla used deceptive language to market Autopilot, California judge rules. Tesla’s sales in California should be suspended for 30 days because its marketing around Autopilot and Full Self-Driving misled consumers, a California administrative law judge has ruled. Back in 2022, the California DMV accused the automaker of using deceptive language to advertise those products and making  it seem like its vehicles are capable of level 5 autonomous driving...
